Work Experience

Doctoral Research Fellow

April 2020 - Present
Oslo, Norway

Center for Molecular medicine Norway (NCMM)

Supervisor: Anthony Mathelier
During my PhD I worked mainly with transcription factor (TF) binding as a mechanism for gene expression regulation. My main project was investigating the co-binding patterns of TFs in multiple species. I developed a pipeline COBIND that uses non-negative matrix factorisation to discover co-bind patterns. I also worked on the updates of JASPAR database as a curator and developer. More importantly, I learned an innumerable amount of things.

Staff engineer

February 2019 - May 2019
Trondheim, Norway

Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU)

I worked on reviewing DNA methylation data analysis approaches and available tools.

Summer researcher

April 2015 - September 2015
Vilnius, Lithuania

Institute of Biotechnology

I was a team member of Vilnius iGEM 2015. Our team created a project called “Coliclock” for iGEM 2015 competition in Boston, USA. In the project we developed a system of time-controlled 'switch-off' of genetically modified E. coli, based on CRISPR-Cas system.
